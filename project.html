Health haul
[National Olympiad finalist]

In December 2020, I participated in a Nationwide Innovation Olympiad from Team Chinimini Express. We worked on The Unorganized Health system of Bangladesh and proposed a holistic application which medical colleges and hospitals can use to keep track of the Teachers, Doctors, Nurses, technicians, Managers, students and patients profiles, Their problems and solutions given. The policy makers can work to structure the system with the data over time. It was a idea generation based olympiad, where me and my team (Team Chinimini Express) proposed for both medical students, healthcare providers and care seekers to come together under a single platform Which would ensure increased awareness, healthcare accessibility, and preparedness of the caregivers. Later, due to the lack of funding, the project could not reach development phase.
