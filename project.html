My task started with research tool preparation, 1:1 data collection, Data entry, data cleaning, Analyzing, and interpreting the results. Health haul
[National finalist]

In December 2020, I participated in a Nationwide Innovation Olympiad from Team Chinimini Express. We worked on The Unorganized Health system of Bangladesh and proposed a holistic application which medical colleges and hospitals can use to keep track of the Teachers, Doctors, Nurses, technicians, Managers, students, and patients are profiled, and their problems and solutions are given. Policy makers can use the data to structure the system over time. It was an idea generation based olympiad, where me and my team (Team Chinimini Express) proposed for both medical students, healthcare providers and care seekers to come together under a single platform Which would ensure increased awareness, healthcare accessibility, and preparedness of the caregivers. Later, due to the lack of funding, the project could not reach development phase.
