Predicting Urban Trends of Growth with Google Earth Engine

In this undergraduate research project, I, along with my team worked on urbanization prediction using the tools: Google Earth Engine (GEE), QGIS, and ArcGIS. First, we obtained remote sensed data from GEE, then, from the Land Cover land Use (LULC) data, we applied several machine learning algorithms and using Mareov chain algorithm, we predicted the urbanization for the year 2020. Then, we calculated evaluation metrics based on the actual data of 2020. Lastly, the prediction for the year 2025 and 2030 was done.
